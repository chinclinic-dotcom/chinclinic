About

Kevin Chin, NP, FNP(C), MS, BScN, HBSc

Owner & Medical Director

NP Kevin Chin is a board-certified Family Nurse Practitioner with advanced training through McMaster University, the University of Toronto, and D’Youville University (New York).

His clinical background includes family medicine, urgent care, and virtual practice, with a focus on comprehensive assessment, diagnosis, treatment, and longitudinal management across a broad range of medical concerns.

Areas of clinical specialty include weight and metabolic management, men’s health, hormone therapy, PCOS, and sports rehab medicine. He has been involved in medical weight management since the early clinical trials and introduction of GLP-1 therapies in Canada and has extensive experience supporting patients through evidence-based metabolic and lifestyle treatment approaches. He is a leading authority across the province on both medical weight management and men’s health treatments.

NP Chin’s approach to care emphasizes thoughtful communication, individualized treatment planning, and medically grounded care tailored to each patient’s goals, preferences, and long-term health outcomes.

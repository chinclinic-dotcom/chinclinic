Weight management & metabolic health

Comprehensive medical assessment and evidence-based treatment for weight, metabolism, and related health concerns. Care is individualized to your history, lifestyle, lab work, and long-term goals, with a focus on sustainable and realistic progress rather than short-term dieting alone.

Treatment plans may include nutritional and lifestyle strategies, exercise guidance, metabolic optimization, and prescription therapies when appropriate, including GLP1 and newer metabolic medications such as Ozempic®, Wegovy®, Mounjaro®, Zepbound®, Retatrutide, and others. Common concerns addressed include obesity, insulin resistance, prediabetes, emotional eating, weight regain, fatigue, and metabolic syndrome.

Ongoing follow-up focuses on safety, accountability, symptom improvement, and long-term maintenance.

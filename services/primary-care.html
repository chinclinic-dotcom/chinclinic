Primary care

Comprehensive virtual primary care for ongoing health needs, preventive screening, and chronic disease management. Care is patient-centred, collaborative, and focused on continuity over time.

Services may include routine check-ins, medication management, blood pressure and diabetes care, preventive screening, women’s and men’s health concerns, mental health support, lab and imaging requisitions, and referrals to publicly funded specialists when appropriate. Visits are conducted securely by video or phone across Ontario.

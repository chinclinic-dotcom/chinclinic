Longevity & lifestyle medicine

A proactive and personalized approach focused on optimizing long-term health, energy, function, and quality of life. Care may include preventive health planning, advanced risk assessment, metabolic optimization, lifestyle interventions, and evidence-based therapies tailored to your goals and stage of life.

Areas commonly addressed include energy, sleep, recovery, stress, body composition, cardiovascular and metabolic health, cognitive performance, healthy aging, and sustainable lifestyle habits. Recommendations are individualized and grounded in current evidence, with a focus on practical strategies that can realistically fit into daily life.

Prescriptions, referrals, devices & forms

Additional medical documentation, requisitions, referrals, and supportive health services tailored to work, school, insurance, and wellness needs.

Services may include referrals to physiotherapy, dietitians, massage therapy, chiropractic care, counselling, or other allied health providers. Imaging and laboratory requisitions can be arranged when medically appropriate. Insured device prescriptions may include orthotics, compression stockings, glucose monitors, blood pressure devices, and related supports.

Medical documentation may include sick notes, school forms, workplace accommodations, insurance paperwork, fitness assessments, and other medical forms. Additional fees may apply depending on the complexity and time required.

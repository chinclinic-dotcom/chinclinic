ADHD — assessment & treatment

Structured virtual ADHD assessment and ongoing management for children, adolescents, or adults. Care includes a detailed clinical history, symptom review, screening tools, and evaluation for contributing or overlapping concerns when appropriate.

Treatment plans are individualized and may include prescription medications, non-medication strategies, behavioural supports, and longitudinal follow-up to monitor effectiveness, tolerability, and functional improvement. Common concerns include focus and concentration difficulties, executive dysfunction, procrastination, emotional regulation, burnout, and workplace or academic performance.

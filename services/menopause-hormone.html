Menopause & hormone therapy

Thoughtful evaluation and longitudinal care for hormonal changes associated with perimenopause and menopause. Care includes symptom assessment, education, treatment planning, and ongoing monitoring to help improve quality of life and day-to-day function.

Common concerns include hot flashes, night sweats, fatigue, sleep disruption, mood changes, brain fog, low libido, urinary symptoms, vaginal dryness or discomfort, and changes in weight or body composition.

Treatment options may include menopausal hormone therapy (MHT/HRT), non-hormonal therapies, lifestyle support, and preventive health discussions tailored to your medical history and preferences.

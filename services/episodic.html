Episodic & walk-in concerns

Convenient virtual assessment and treatment for common acute medical concerns that do not necessarily require an in-person visit. Timely appointments are often available.

Common concerns include urinary tract infections (UTIs), rashes, eczema, cold and flu symptoms, sinus infections, conjunctivitis (pink eye), bug bites, mild skin infections, medication refills, acne, seasonal allergies, and other everyday medical concerns. Prescriptions, requisitions, and follow-up planning are provided as appropriate through secure video or phone appointments.

Chin Family Clinic provides comprehensive primary and specialty care through secure virtual visits. Thoughtful, unhurried consultations with clear plans and follow-up — for patients in Ontario and British Columbia.

How care works

  • Book online based on your concern
  • Visits by secure video or phone
  • Clear assessment, plan, and documentation
  • Prescriptions, referrals, and investigations arranged as needed
  • Follow-up aligned with your condition and goals

Who we help

  • Patients without a family provider
  • Individuals seeking timely access to care
  • Those managing ongoing or complex conditions
  • Patients looking for a more personalized, thorough, and unhurried approach

Home visits are available in select regions — contact the clinic for details.

Services

Primary, episodic, and specialty care — structured as a modern private clinic.

Specialty care

Specialized areas where patients often benefit from more time, depth, and expert management:

  • Weight Management & Metabolic Health
  • Longevity & Lifestyle Medicine
  • Menopause and Hormone Therapy
  • Men’s Health including testosterone
  • PCOS
  • ADHD
  • Chronic fatigue
  • Skin & hair concerns
  • Sports Medicine
  • Travel medicine and consultations

Primary & episodic care

Examples include:

  • Urinary tract infections
  • Rashes and skin conditions
  • Cold and flu symptoms
  • Sinus infections
  • Conjunctivitis (pink eye)
  • Birth control and contraceptive care

Plus ongoing condition management, preventive care and screening, and other general medical concerns by video or phone.

Medical services

  • Prescription renewals
  • Specialist referrals
  • Referrals to allied health (physiotherapy, dietitians, therapists)
  • Imaging requisitions (i.e. X-ray, ultrasound, MRI)
  • Insurance and medical forms
  • Prescriptions for insured medical devices (e.g., orthotics, compression stockings)

Our approach

Care here is structured, thoughtful, and patient-centred.

We focus on understanding your concerns, explaining options clearly, and developing plans that are both realistic and medically sound. Whether addressing a single issue or providing ongoing care, visits are designed to be focused, respectful, and clinically thorough.

The goal is simple: clear answers, appropriate care, and continuity you can rely on.

About

Kevin Chin, NP, FNP(C), MS, BScN, HBSc
Founder & Medical Director

NP Kevin Chin is a board-certified Family Nurse Practitioner with training through McMaster University, the University of Toronto, and D’Youville University (New York).

His clinical background includes family medicine, community care, and virtual practice, with a focus on comprehensive assessment, diagnosis, and longitudinal management.

He provides care across the clinic’s core areas, including metabolic health, hormone therapy, ADHD, dermatology, and general primary care.

His approach emphasizes clear communication, evidence-based treatment, and care aligned with each patient’s goals.

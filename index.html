About

At Chin Family Clinic, care is built around a patient-centred philosophy: we take time to listen, explain options in plain language, and involve you in decisions about investigations and treatment. Our goal is respectful, empathic encounters — whether you are here for a single concern or ongoing management — with clear follow-up and expectations.

The practice is independent and virtual-first, serving patients in Ontario and British Columbia, with attention to continuity, safety, and communication across every visit type we offer.

Kevin Chin, NP — Founder & Medical Director

Kevin is a board-certified Family Nurse Practitioner specialized in primary care. He graduated from and holds degrees and clinical training through McMaster University, the University of Toronto, and D’Youville University in New York — with continued focus on advanced assessment, diagnosis, therapeutics, and longitudinal care.

He is licensed to practise as a Nurse Practitioner in Ontario and British Columbia. His background spans family medicine clinics, community home care, and modern virtual medicine — experience he applies to patient-centred, empathic visits across the clinic’s clinical interests, including weight management, men’s health, hormone therapy, PCOS, longevity and functional medicine, ADHD, dermatologic concerns, and sports medicine.

In every encounter, the aim is the same: clear explanations, shared decisions, and care that respects your context and goals.
